RULE ID: AML-RULE-002
RULE NAME: Third-Party Funds / Unknown or Undocumented Source of Funds
RISK LEVEL: HIGH

DEFINITION
Flag as suspicious if the customer indicates that funds are not their own, originate from multiple unrelated third parties, or cannot be explained and documented in a consistent way.

TRIGGER CONDITIONS (TEXT INDICATORS)
- Customer says funds come from friends, partners, associates, or clients without contracts/invoices.
- Customer cannot identify senders/beneficiaries clearly.
- Customer cannot provide documentation for source of funds (salary proof, invoices, contracts).
- Customer says sources vary or are unknown.

AML RATIONALE
Third-party ownership and undocumented source of funds prevents KYC verification and is a core AML red flag.

ACTION / OUTCOME
- Increase risk rating.
- Apply EDD.
- Require documentary evidence of source of funds and transaction purpose.
