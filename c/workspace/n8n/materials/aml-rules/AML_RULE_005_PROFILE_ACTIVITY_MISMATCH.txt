RULE ID: AML-RULE-005
RULE NAME: Customer Profile vs Expected Activity Mismatch
RISK LEVEL: MEDIUM to HIGH (depending on severity)

DEFINITION
Flag as suspicious if expected transaction volume, transfer frequency, or cross-border activity is much higher than the customer's stated employment, income, or economic profile. 

TRIGGER CONDITIONS (TEXT INDICATORS)
- Customer is unemployed or low-income but expects high monthly volume.
- Customer states salary is unrelated to expected transfers.
- Customer expects frequent transfers without a credible business explanation.


AML RATIONALE
Mismatch between customer profile and high expected activity is a standard AML risk indicator and may indicate mule usage or hidden third-party business.

ACTION / OUTCOME
- Increase risk score.
- Request documentation (income proof, business documents).
- Escalate if mismatch is significant.
