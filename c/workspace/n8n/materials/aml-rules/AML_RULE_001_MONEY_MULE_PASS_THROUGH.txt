RULE ID: AML-RULE-001
RULE NAME: Money Mule / Pass-Through Account Behavior
RISK LEVEL: CRITICAL (auto-escalate)

DEFINITION
Flag as suspected money laundering if the customer states or implies they will receive funds and forward them to other persons/entities, acting as an intermediary rather than the final beneficiary.

TRIGGER CONDITIONS (TEXT INDICATORS)
- Customer says they will forward, redistribute, or send onward money received from others.
- Customer describes themselves as an intermediary, channel, or middleman.
- Customer indicates other people will use their account.
- Customer indicates they will handle payments for friends/partners/associates.


AML RATIONALE
Pass-through behavior is a primary indicator of money mule activity and layering. This is strongly associated with laundering and fraud proceeds.

ACTION / OUTCOME
- Label as AML suspicious.
- Escalate to Compliance / EDD.
- Request proof of source of funds and proof of beneficiary purpose.
