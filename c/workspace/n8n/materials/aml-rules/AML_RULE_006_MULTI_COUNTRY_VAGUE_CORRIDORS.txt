RULE ID: AML-RULE-006
RULE NAME: Multiple Countries / High-Risk Corridors with Vague Purpose
RISK LEVEL: MEDIUM to HIGH

DEFINITION
Flag as suspicious if the customer expects cross-border transfers involving multiple jurisdictions (especially if described vaguely) without clear counterparties, contracts, or business justification.

TRIGGER CONDITIONS (TEXT INDICATORS)
- Customer lists several countries or says "multiple" countries.
- Customer cannot explain counterparties or business reason for cross-border flows.
- Customer expects frequent cross-border movement combined with other red flags.

AML RATIONALE
Cross-border complexity increases laundering risk and makes source of funds and beneficiary tracing more difficult.

ACTION / OUTCOME
- Increase risk rating.
- Apply EDD if combined with other red flags.
- Require documentation for counterparties and transaction purpose.
