RULE ID: AML-RULE-003
RULE NAME: Rapid Movement / Same-Day Forwarding / Cash-Out Pattern
RISK LEVEL: HIGH

DEFINITION
Flag as suspicious if the customer expects to move money immediately after receiving it, especially if the stated plan is same-day forwarding or immediate cash withdrawal.

TRIGGER CONDITIONS (TEXT INDICATORS)
- Customer states they will forward funds shortly after receipt.
- Customer states they will withdraw cash immediately after incoming transfers.
- Customer indicates rapid movement is normal for their expected activity.

AML RATIONALE
Rapid movement is typical of layering and mule activity. Cash extraction is a high-risk laundering endpoint.

ACTION / OUTCOME
- Label as suspicious.
- Escalate for EDD and transaction monitoring.
- Request explanation and supporting documentation.
