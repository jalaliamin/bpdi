RULE ID: AML-RULE-004
RULE NAME: Avoiding Scrutiny / Avoiding Banks / Avoiding Attention
RISK LEVEL: CRITICAL

DEFINITION
Flag as suspected money laundering if the customer explicitly states that the purpose of the account or transfers is to avoid attention, scrutiny, or bank controls.

TRIGGER CONDITIONS (TEXT INDICATORS)
- Customer states the purpose is to avoid attention or scrutiny.
- Customer states they prefer not to use banks directly.
- Customer expresses intent to avoid monitoring.

AML RATIONALE
This indicates direct intent to evade AML controls and is strongly indicative of laundering behavior.

ACTION / OUTCOME
- Auto-escalate to Compliance immediately.
- Label as AML suspicious.
- Consider rejection or account closure according to bank policy.
